Conversations

WhatsApp Conversation is a 24-hour messaging window that opens when a business contacts a user. A conversation begins when a business either initiates a message or replies to a user. All incoming user messages are FREE, and charges apply only when the business responds.

Unlimited Free Service Conversations

Every business using ConnectStream gets Unlimited FREE service conversations! Businesses can respond to user messages without any charges—it's absolutely FREE.

Marketing Conversation

Marketing Conversations cover all promotional messages, offers, and product updates. These conversations cost ₹0.88 per conversation (for Indian users).

Utility Conversation

Utility Conversations are transactional messages like delivery updates, receipts, and reminders. These conversations cost ₹0.125 per conversation (for Indian users).

Authentication Conversation

Authentication Conversations are used for verification messages like OTP, account registration, and recovery. These conversations cost ₹0.125 per conversation (for Indian users).

Service Conversation

Service Conversations include all user-initiated chats for customer support, chatbot assistance, and queries. These conversations are completely FREE for all users.
